Intro

Agara Mudhal Trust is a registered Indian Non Government, Non Religious, Non Profit, Social Service Organization working on facilitating child education across Tamilnadu particulary through Government schools.

Established in the year of 2018, the organization is devoted in looking at the wider picture; not only to help one cause but to focus on related causes in the long run. We hope by doing this there will be a positive impact on the community and encourage others to make a difference.

We started our first project by providing necessary infra structure in a Government run school at Siruthamur in Chengalpat District. Since then we are taking up other projects across Tamil Nadu with a hope of steadily expanding our support to cover many needy schools.
