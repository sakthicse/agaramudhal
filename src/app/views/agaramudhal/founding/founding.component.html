Founding
  • Agaramudhal was founded and established in the year 2018 by like minded friends of GCE, Salem 1990 batch.
  • A spark lit during a casual WhatsApp chat resulted in the formation Agaramudhal.
  • Since then with the unstinted support from the frieds, the trust started doing its charity work.
  • The journey is towards aiding and providing the needed infra structure facility in Govt Schools at remote places.
