Singililan Kombai

Our Third Project for Panchayat Union Primary School, Singililan Kombai, Namakkal Dt.

Challenges / Needs

  1. Students were facing difficulties in getting quality drinking water.
  2. Students were also facing another challenge due to absence of running water facility in the toilets.
  3. Flooring in girls toilets were found damaged making it uneven and urinals for boys were found made of plastic cans.
  4. Sufficient number of fans were missing in class rooms resulting in sweating of a section students

Implemented Solutions

  1. A 1000L PVC overhead tank was provided for the school to store the water by tapping from the pipe line of Panchayat Overhead tank and Bore well of the school. Distribution line from the overhead tank with an UV filter was provided so that the students could get the drinking water.
  2. From the same overhead tank, pipelines were laid to water the toilets.
  3. Vitrified tiling was provided in girls toilets. Both boys and girls toilets were painted making them neat and clean. Wash basins were also provided. New porcelain urinals were installed in boys toilet by replacing the plastic can urinals.
  4. Two additional fans were provided to ensure sufficient air flow through out the class rooms.

Results

A happy and relieved teachers and students resulting in increased focus on teaching and learning.
